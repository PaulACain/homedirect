Honest answers

What if the AI gets my negotiation wrong?

Our AI models are trained on millions of transactions and real-time Tampa Bay comps. Every counter-offer comes with the data behind it — so you see exactly why we're recommending each move. You always review and approve before anything is submitted.

Is this even legal and compliant?

Yes. HomeDirectAI operates under a licensed real estate brokerage. All transactions follow Florida state law. Our document generation is reviewed by licensed real estate attorneys and updated with every regulatory change.

Won't the buyer's agent take advantage of me being unrepresented?

Our AI negotiation engine is purpose-built for this. It doesn't have a relationship with the buyer's agent, no incentive to close fast, and no fear of conflict. It analyzes the offer against current comps and recommends the optimal response — consistently.

What if something goes wrong at 10pm before closing?

Your transaction hub includes escalation pathways to licensed support — not a chatbot. Critical pre-closing issues are handled by real people backed by AI tools, not the reverse. You're never alone in your deal.

FSBO homes sell for less — won't this be the same?

This is the most important distinction: HomeDirectAI is not FSBO. FSBO homes sell for less because sellers negotiate alone, without data, and often panic. Our AI negotiation engine gives you the same comp analysis and strategic leverage a top agent would use — at 1% the cost.
