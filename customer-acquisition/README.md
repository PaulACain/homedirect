# HomeDirectAI — Customer Acquisition Strategy

Tampa Bay, FL · April 2026

---

## Overview

HomeDirectAI serves **three distinct audiences**, each requiring a separate marketing campaign, messaging framework, and hero journey. The brand color system and psychology are unified across all three — only the narrative and channels shift.

| Audience | Primary Goal | Key Emotion | Primary Channel |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Buyers** | Search, tour, and offer without a buyer's agent | Empowerment + Savings | Meta Ads, SEM, Reddit |
| **Sellers** | List and close at 1% — keep the commission | Righteous indignation → control | Google Search, YouTube, Facebook |
| **Concierges** | Earn $20/showing facilitating home tours | Dignity + earnings upgrade | TikTok, Facebook Gig Groups, Indeed |

## The Three Hero Journeys at a Glance

### Buyer
> **Act 1:** Locked out — needs an agent just to see a house, flying to Tampa for showings, overpaying for buyer's agent they didn't choose.
> **Act 2:** Discovers HomeDirectAI — $20 chaperone unlocks any door, AI surfaces comps, digital offer in real time.
> **Act 3:** Closes on a Tampa home they found, toured, and won — without ever being in the back of an agent's car.

### Seller
> **Act 1:** Sticker shock — $23,400 in commissions on a $430K home. Just found out they were paying the buyer's agent too.
> **Act 2:** Discovers the third option — not risky FSBO, not a discount broker, but AI-powered full service at 1%.
> **Act 3:** Closes with $19,000 more in their pocket. The AI negotiated. The chaperone handled showings. They stayed in control the whole time.

### Concierge
> **Act 1:** Stuck in food delivery — vehicle wearing down, income unpredictable, no dignity, no skill-building.
> **Act 2:** Discovers chaperone work — $20/showing, 30-45 minutes, daylight hours, their Tampa neighborhood knowledge finally has a dollar value.
> **Act 3:** Earning $800-$1,200/month supplement, building real estate expertise, introducing themselves as a "home showing specialist."
